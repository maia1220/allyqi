Framing Eighteen Coils in Cerebral Aneurysms Trial

Title Abbreviation: FEAT
Condition or Disease: Cerebral Aneurysm

This trial is being conducted in order to compare angiographic outcomes in patients receiving 0.014-0.0155" platinum framing and filling coils (larger diameter coils) versus those treated solely with coils less than 0.014" (with a standard diameter). Hypothesis: Angiographic occlusion at follow-up imaging will be more frequent in patients receiving 0.014-0.0155" platinum coils during embolization compared to those receiving smaller-diameter coils. More information is in the following website: ClinicalTrials.gov.

Role Data monitoring, data analysis, project management assistance, site communication, reporting site and study performance to study PI

Data Innovations Transforming the typical PowerPoint slides into the new dashboards using Power BI, which also incorporate the query resolution data from REDCap to display the site enrollment and data performance, and to reduce the manual work to create PowerPoint slides each month.

Comparative Analysis of the Entegrion Point of Care Coagulation Monitor (PCM) With ROTEM in the Measurement of Viscoelastic Coagulation Parameters

Title Abbreviation: PCM
Condition or Disease: Hemostasis Monitoring

The primary aim of this study is a performance evaluation of the PCM coagulation monitor in comparison to the ROTEM thromboelastometry system. To include a measurement of the entire range of the test system, subjects with a medical management need that requires the measurement of their hemostasis status (such as PT/PTT, INR) or are at risk for being hypo-coagulable or hyper-coagulable will be included in this study. ClinicalTrials.gov.

Role Data monitoring, data analysis, project management assistance, site communication, reporting site and study performance

Data Innovations Building a Microsoft Access database to track data monitoring progress, which facilitates the reporting process for site performance.
