Introduction

Clinical Trials Manager with an M.S. in Public Health, a certificate in Data Analytics and Visualizations from Vanderbilt University, and 5+ years of experience in clinical operations and data management. Expertise in transforming clinical trials management using programming languages and data visualization tools, such as STATA, Python, R Markdown, and PowerBi. Currently supporting the design and conduct of operational activities related to planning, implementing, and reporting of multicenter clinical trials in accordance with Standard Operating Procedures, Good Clinical Practice, and local and federal regulations.
